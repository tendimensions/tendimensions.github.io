About Me

By day I run Ten Dimensional Software, an AI strategy and software consulting business. I work with C-suite executives and their leadership teams to navigate the AI hype, build vendor-agnostic strategies, and turn AI ambitions into measurable business outcomes. It's an extraordinary moment — trillion-dollar bets being made in all directions, predictions all over the map, and a pace of innovation the world has never seen before.

By evenings and weekends, I'm a hobbyist developer. I build personal projects across a range of stacks — mobile apps, Discord bots, book cataloging systems — because building software is genuinely one of my favorite things to do. The tools and techniques that LLMs have opened the door to make this an exciting time to be a developer.

I also serve as Treasurer on the Board of Trustees at the Tuxedo Park Public Library and as a Commissioner and IT Administrator for the Tuxedo Joint Fire District, managing the district's technology infrastructure.
